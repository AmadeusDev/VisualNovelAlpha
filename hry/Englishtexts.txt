As the Sun, even my doing in this town will have an end which shall leave devastating result.
Many people would say that this distant future is absurdly far away from them.
How many times did you try to convince yourself with a sentence: „I still have enough time.“ and assure your calamitous soul  of the contrary of the truth? 
I closed my book and stood up because my station where I live was getting closer.
As the train was stopping I started to realize the importance of this situation and with grinding sound of the breaks the nightmare, which is ahead of me continued.
The door opened and a night breeze flushed these thoughts away into the waters of oblivion.
I felt like a newborn child and my first visit to this town is going to be the remote library on the top of the town.
